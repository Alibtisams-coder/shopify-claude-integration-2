01  ·  Brand identity

The name, the mark,
the meaning.

FELŌRA (fel·OR·ah) — from the Latin ferre (to carry) and flora (to bloom). The macron over the Ō is intentional. A small mark of distinction that signals immediately: this is not ordinary. This is considered. This is premium.

The wordmark is set in Cormorant Garamond at tracking .5em — wide-spaced, unhurried, confident. Paired with Jost for all body copy: clean, modern, quiet authority.

04  ·  Our woman

She is not a demographic.
She is a feeling.

FELŌRA exists for one woman — and she wears many faces. She is the first-time mama terrified and overjoyed. The career woman who won't slow down. The exhausted second-timer who knows what she needs. She is between 20 and 40. She shops on her phone at midnight. And she deserves better than the clinical products currently offered to her.

i
Who she is
Pregnant women & new mothers, ages 20–40, primarily USA. She values quality, emotion, and brand story. She buys products that make her feel seen.
ii
What she feels
Back pain. Pelvic pressure. Exhaustion. Swelling. A body she no longer recognises. She is doing something extraordinary and she feels completely alone in it.
iii
What she wants
To feel held. To feel beautiful. To find products that speak to the sacredness of what her body is doing — not products that treat her like a medical condition.
