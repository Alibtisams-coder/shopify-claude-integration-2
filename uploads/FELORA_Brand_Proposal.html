01
Brand overview

What FELŌRA is.

FELŌRA is a premium direct-to-consumer women's wellness brand founded in 2026. We design, source and sell comfort and recovery products for women during pregnancy, postpartum and beyond — products that are doctor informed, pregnancy safe and built around how her body actually feels, not around a specification sheet.

We are not a maternity product company. We are the brand every woman reaches for the moment she finds out she is carrying life — and we grow with her through every season of womanhood that follows.

"Most brands treat pregnancy as a condition to manage. FELŌRA treats it as something to honour."

We launched with a single hero product — The Carry Belt™ — a premium pregnancy support belt priced at £55, positioned against a clinical and generic market. FELŌRA's competitive advantage is not the product itself. It is the brand wrapped around it — the story, the voice, the aesthetic, and the emotional connection that no clinical competitor can replicate.

01
Primary market
United Kingdom — targeting women aged 25–40 who are pregnant or in early postpartum recovery and who aspire to premium wellness products.
02
Secondary market
Canada and the United States — entering after UK reviews and social proof are established. Long-term primary market.

Mission · Vision · Values

Why we exist.

Our mission: To be the brand every woman reaches for the moment she finds out she is carrying life.

Our vision: A world where every pregnant and postpartum woman has access to wellness products that honour the extraordinary nature of her journey — not just manage its symptoms.

"We will never make a product that is not worthy of this moment. We will never use a word that does not honour her journey. We will never treat pregnancy as something to manage — when it is something to celebrate."

Our core values — the five things FELŌRA will never compromise on:

  • Honesty. We only promise what we can deliver. We never use inflated claims, fake reviews or misleading copy. Our brand is built on truth.
  • Intentionality. Every product decision, every word, every design choice begins with one question — does this make her feel held?
  • Premium without pretension. FELŌRA is aspirational but never exclusionary. She deserves luxury — regardless of her background.
  • She first. Every metric, every decision, every campaign is measured against one standard — is this good for her?
  • Continuous growth. FELŌRA is a brand in motion. We listen to our customers, improve our products and evolve our story — always forward.

Brand identity

How FELŌRA looks.

FELŌRA's visual identity is built around three principles: restraint, warmth and precision. Nothing is accidental. Nothing is generic. Every visual element is chosen to make her feel that she has arrived somewhere that was made entirely for her.

Typography

Cormorant Garamond
Headlines · brand name · pull quotes · prices · all emotional statements. Weights 300 and 400 only. Italic for gold accent phrases.
Jost
Body text · labels · navigation · buttons · all functional copy. Weights 300 and 400. Uppercase with letter-spacing for labels and eyebrows.

Design rules — non-negotiable:

  • No border radius anywhere. Sharp edges only. Rounded corners are generic. Sharpness signals confidence.
  • No emojis in any brand communication. Ever.
  • No .99 pricing. £55 not £54.99. Premium brands do not play pricing tricks.
  • Gold is used sparingly. It is an accent, not a background. Its power comes from restraint.
  • Whitespace is content. FELŌRA never crowds. Every section breathes.

Brand voice & tone

How FELŌRA speaks.

FELŌRA speaks like a trusted friend who understands her body better than any clinical product ever has. We are warm without being saccharine. Premium without being cold. Direct without being aggressive. Emotional without being manipulative.

We speak to her — not about her, not about our products, not about features. Every sentence asks: does this make her feel seen?

FELŌRA says
  • You deserve to feel held.
  • The 2am moment. Finally answered.
  • She should not have to choose.
  • Your body is doing something extraordinary.
  • We built this for exactly this moment.
FELŌRA never says
  • Reduces pain by 70%
  • Don't compromise on comfort
  • Add to bag 🛍️
  • Limited time offer!
  • Designed by mamas for mamas

The four tones of FELŌRA voice:

i
Empathetic
We acknowledge her reality — the pain, the exhaustion, the invisible weight — before we offer anything. She feels seen before she sees the product.
ii
Poetic
FELŌRA uses language with intention. "The 2am moment" says more than "nighttime back pain." Choose the image over the explanation. Always.
iii
Confident
We never hedge. "This might help" becomes "This was built for exactly this." Confidence is not arrogance — it is knowing what we made and who we made it for.
iv
Intimate
We write as if we are writing to one person. Not "mamas everywhere" — to her specifically. Singular. Personal. Present tense.

Target customer

She who carries life.

Her name is not a demographic. She is not a data point. She is the woman at 2am who cannot sleep because her back aches and nothing has truly helped. She is the professional who wears a support belt under her work clothes because she refuses to stop living her life. She is the first-time mama who wants everything around this extraordinary experience to feel as sacred as the experience itself.

Primary customer
  • Age: 25–38
  • Location: UK · Canada · US
  • Stage: Second or third trimester · early postpartum
  • Income: Middle to upper-middle · values quality over price
  • Mindset: She expects premium. She has researched. She trusts brands that speak to her authentically.
  • Finds us: Instagram · TikTok · word of mouth · Google search
Secondary customer
  • The gift buyer — her friend, sister, partner or mother buying for a baby shower or pregnancy milestone
  • The postpartum buyer — she used the belt during pregnancy and returns for recovery products
  • The brand evangelist — she loved FELŌRA so much she tells every pregnant woman she meets

FELŌRA's customer does not just buy a product. She joins a brand whose entire existence is dedicated to making her feel honoured during the most extraordinary season of her life. That emotional connection is what drives repeat purchase, word-of-mouth and the reviews that build the brand's future.
